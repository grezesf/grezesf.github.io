Research Interests:

My research focuses on Neural Networks architectures, usually used for Natural Language Processing.
My current focus is on Adversarial Attacks and Defenses, a recent development in Neural Networks.
My current project aims to provide Neural Network researchers with a simple tool to fight certain adversarial attacks.
In the past I have worked on Binary Neural Networks for Speech Recognition, GANs for text data, Reservoir Computing, Audio Source Separation using Neural Networks, Acoustic Prosody, Computational Biology.
